The three sisters are ___fill this in___

  • Corn
  • Beans
  • Squash

Corn is planted first.

After a few weeks, it has grown enough to act a natural trellis for the beans.

  • Vitamin C
  • Magnesium
  • Fiber

Beans are planted next.

They add support as they grow up the corn stalks. They also absorb and process nitrogen to fertilize the soil.

  • Fiber
  • Amino Acids

Squash __________fill this in___________

Broad squash leaves shade the ground and keep animals out. As they wilt and fall off the plant, they add nutrients to the soil.

  • Vitamin C
  • Magnesum
  • Manganese
Some cultures include a fourth sister-- tobacco, sunflower, amaranth
